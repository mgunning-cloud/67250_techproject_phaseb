Exhibitions

Exhibition 1: Art by Society

The "Art by Society" exhibition explores the relationship between art and society, showcasing works that reflect social, political, and cultural themes. This exhibition features a diverse range of artworks, including paintings, sculptures, and multimedia installations, created by artists from around the world. Visitors can expect to see thought-provoking pieces that challenge traditional notions of art and encourage reflection on contemporary issues.

Exhibition 2: Modern Masters

The "Modern Masters" exhibition showcases the works of renowned artists from the 20th century, highlighting their contributions to the evolution of modern art. This exhibition features a selection of paintings, drawings, and sculptures that demonstrate the innovative techniques and unique styles that defined this transformative period in art history.
